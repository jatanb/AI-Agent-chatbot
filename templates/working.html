How Scheme Scout Works

Advanced AI pipeline to find your perfect opportunity

Step 1: Natural Language Processing 🧠

You type a query in plain English. Our AI model processes it to understand:

  • What type of opportunity you're looking for (job, internship, scheme, etc.)
  • Your experience level (fresher, mid-level, senior)
  • Preferred role, location, and company
  • Any special requirements (remote, part-time, contract)

Step 2: Intent Classification 🎯

The system classifies your query into categories:

  • Job Search: Traditional employment opportunities
  • Internship Hunt: Internship and trainee programs
  • Government Schemes: Govt jobs and skill programs
  • General Inquiry: Information and guidance

Step 3: Named Entity Recognition 📋

Our NER model extracts key information from your query:

  • Job titles and roles (Software Engineer, Data Scientist, etc.)
  • Locations (Cities, states, or remote)
  • Technical skills (Python, JavaScript, AWS, etc.)
  • Company names and industries
  • Experience requirements

Step 4: Multi-Source Search 🔍

Query is sent across multiple job platforms simultaneously:

  • LinkedIn
  • Naukri.com
  • Internshala
  • Indeed
  • Glassdoor
  • Government portals (Naukri.gov)
  • Specialized job boards

Step 5: Result Aggregation & Deduplication ⚡

Combine results from all sources and remove duplicates:

  • Parse and standardize data from different sources
  • Remove duplicate listings
  • Extract key information (title, company, salary, deadline)
  • Prepare data for ranking

Step 6: Cross-Encoder Reranking 🏆

Advanced ML model scores relevance of each opportunity:

  • Uses transformer-based neural networks
  • Considers query semantics and opportunity content
  • Scores each result on relevance (0-1)
  • Sorts by relevance score

Step 7: Semantic Re-ranking 🧬

Final pass uses semantic embeddings for quality assurance:

  • Generate embeddings for query and results
  • Calculate semantic similarity
  • Reorder top results by semantic relevance
  • Return top 5 most relevant opportunities

Step 8: Delivery & Caching 📦

Results are delivered instantly with smart caching:

  • Commonly searched queries are cached for instant results
  • Personalized results based on your profile
  • Chat history maintained for context
  • Results show title, company, salary, deadline, apply link

Why This Approach? 💡

Unlike traditional job boards, Scheme Scout:

  • Understands Context: Your query is analyzed semantically, not just keyword-matched
  • Aggregates Data: Search across 50+ platforms in one query
  • Eliminates Noise: Intelligent ranking removes irrelevant results
  • Saves Time: What takes hours of manual browsing is done in seconds
  • Personalization: Results adapt based on your profile and search history
  • Privacy: All processing is secure and your data is never shared

Average Processing Time: < 2 seconds ⚡

That includes searching 50+ sources, aggregating results, and intelligent ranking!
